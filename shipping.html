How can I track my order?

You'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS once your order is shipped. You can:

  • Use the tracking number on our website
  • Click the tracking link in your email

What if I'm not home during delivery?

Don't worry! We have multiple options:

  • Two delivery attempts will be made
  • Option to reschedule delivery time
  • Delivery to alternate address
  • Pick up from nearest collection point

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within Bangladesh. However:

  • Priority countries: India, Nepal, Singapore
  • Join waitlist for international shipping

Can I change my delivery address?

Yes, you can modify your delivery address:

  • Before shipping: Edit in your account
  • After shipping: Contact support within 12 hours
  • Additional fee may apply for route change
